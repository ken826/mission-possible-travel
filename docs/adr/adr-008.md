# ADR-008: Structured Document and Invoice Management

Status: Accepted
Date: 2026-01-09

## Context

Invoices, itineraries, and receipts are currently scattered across inboxes and shared folders.

## Decision

All documents will be uploaded and stored directly against the request, with structured metadata.

## Attachment Types

QUOTE
ITINERARY
INVOICE
RECEIPT
OTHER

Optional metadata includes supplier, invoice number, amount, and due date.

## Consequences

### Positive

- Faster finance reconciliation
- Improved audit readiness
- No reliance on email storage

### Negative

- Requires retention and access policies
