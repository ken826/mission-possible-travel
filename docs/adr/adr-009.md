# ADR-009: Role-Based Access Control and Auditing

Status: Accepted
Date: 2026-01-09

## Context

The system must support internal segregation of duties and potential future external access.

## Decision

Implement role-based access control with full auditing of sensitive actions.

## Initial Roles

EMPLOYEE
OPS_COORDINATOR
APPROVER
FINANCE
PARTNER (future)

All approvals, overrides, and document uploads are auditable.

## Consequences

### Positive

- Enables safe expansion
- Supports compliance and trust

### Negative

- Slightly higher upfront design effort
